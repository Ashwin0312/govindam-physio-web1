Who We Are

Dedicated to restoring your movement and your life.

We are a highly advanced physiotherapy and rehabilitation clinic in Bijainagar, committed to evidence-based care, precise diagnostics, and getting you back to doing what you love without pain.

Our Philosophy

Treating the root cause, not just the symptom.

At Govindam Physiotherapy, we believe that pain is a signal, not a lifestyle. For years, we have served the community with a simple but powerful mission: to provide world-class, individualized rehabilitation that goes beyond temporary relief.

"We don't believe in one-size-fits-all treatments. Every body is different, and every recovery plan should be too."

From complex neurological rehabilitation to acute sports injuries and chronic back pain, we combine state-of-the-art modalities with expert hands-on manual therapy. Our goal is to educate, empower, and heal our patients so they can reclaim their mobility and confidence.

Meet Your Physiotherapist

Dr.Govind Nishan

Lead Physiotherapist & Founder

Dr. Govind Nishan (BPT, MIAP, CMS ED & ET, CKTT) is a dedicated and experienced physiotherapist committed to helping patients live pain-free and active lives. He specializes in treating joint pain, neurological conditions, and post-surgical rehabilitation with personalized care. His approach focuses on understanding each patient’s needs and delivering effective, result-oriented treatment. With a blend of modern techniques and compassionate care, he aims to restore mobility, strength, and overall well-being.
