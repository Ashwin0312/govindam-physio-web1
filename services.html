Physiotherapy
Core Service

Pain is often your body's way of asking for attention — and physiotherapy is the answer. We combine hands-on manual therapy with targeted exercise programs to restore the movement you've been missing. Whether you're recovering from surgery, dealing with chronic pain, or bouncing back from a sports injury, our sessions are built around your body and your pace.

Post-surgery rehab Chronic pain Sports injuries Manual therapy
02 / 06
Chiropractic Treatment
Alignment & Spine

When your spine is out of alignment, everything else tends to follow. Chiropractic care at Govindam focuses on precise, gentle adjustments that take pressure off the nervous system and let your body function the way it's meant to. Many patients walk out feeling lighter after their very first session.

Spinal adjustment Neck & back pain Posture correction Nerve relief
03 / 06
Cupping Therapy
Recovery & Circulation

An ancient technique, refined for modern recovery. Cupping creates gentle suction on the skin to lift fascia, increase blood flow to tight, stubborn areas, and help your body flush out what's been building up. It's especially effective for athletes and people who carry stress in their upper back.

Muscle recovery Fascia release Improved circulation Inflammation
04 / 06
Needling Therapy
Trigger Point Release

Some muscle knots simply refuse to let go — no matter how much you stretch or massage. That's where dry needling comes in. A very fine needle is inserted directly into the trigger point, causing it to relax almost immediately. The results can be remarkably fast for stubborn pain.

Deep muscle tension Trigger points Headache relief Myofascial pain
05 / 06
Taping
Support & Stabilisation

Applied correctly, kinesiology taping supports muscles and joints without restricting movement, reduces swelling, and gives your body the proprioceptive feedback it needs to move better. We use taping both as a standalone treatment and as part of broader rehabilitation.

Joint support Swelling reduction Injury prevention Kinesiology
06 / 06
Ergonomics
Prevention & Workplace Health

A lot of the pain people bring to us started at a desk. We assess your workspace and habits, then give you practical, specific changes that reduce strain and stop the injury cycle before it begins. Prevention isn't glamorous, but it's highly effective.

Workspace assessment Posture coaching Repetitive strain Injury prevention
How It Works

Your path to recovery, step by step

Assessment

We begin by listening. A thorough clinical evaluation — movement, history, pain patterns — helps us understand what's actually happening, not just what it looks like on the surface.

Planning

No two people are alike, so no two plans should be. We build a personalised treatment roadmap with clear milestones, realistic timelines, and regular check-ins.

Treatment

Hands-on therapy, guided exercises, advanced modalities — everything we have goes into your sessions. We adjust as you progress, because recovery rarely moves in a straight line.
